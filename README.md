# GECAP  
**Advanced and Predictive Clinical Management**

GECAP is a smart digital platform designed to optimize appointment scheduling and daily operations at healthcare facilities.

More than just a calendar, GECAP is a comprehensive tool that improves time efficiency without creating additional administrative burdens.

The system incorporates automated alerts, smart reminders, and absence prediction models. Additionally, it allows patients to access their medical records, download receipts, and stay connected with their healthcare facility and/or providers.

It also includes voice dictation features for clinical documentation, reducing operational times and improving biosafety measures by minimizing contact with devices.

GECAP is specifically designed for primary care centers, offering a simple, efficient, and accessible solution that improves both internal management and the patient experience.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

**Smart Alerts**  
  Automatic notifications for clinical staff.

**Absence Prediction**  
  Smart algorithms that optimize the schedule and maximize profitability.

**Voice Dictation**  
  Quick recording of progress notes and medical records via voice recognition, reducing the risk of equipment contamination.

**Schedule Management**  
  Full control over offices, exam rooms, and specialist schedules.
